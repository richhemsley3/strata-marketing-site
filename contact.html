COMMON QUESTIONS

Before you reach out

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

How long does a typical demo take?

Most demos run about 30 minutes. We'll tailor the session to your specific data stack and security requirements, so you see exactly how Strata would work in your environment.

Do I need to prepare anything before the demo?

Not at all. Just bring your questions. If you'd like a more tailored session, sharing your current data sources or compliance requirements in the form helps us prepare a more relevant walkthrough.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes. After your demo, you can start a 14-day free trial with full platform access. No credit card required, and our team will help you get set up.

What if I just have a quick question?

Email us directly at hello@strata.io or use the general inquiry form above. Our team typically responds within 2 hours during business hours.

Do you offer enterprise pricing?

Yes. For teams with more than 50 users or custom compliance requirements, we offer tailored enterprise plans. Request a demo and mention your needs — we'll connect you with the right team.
