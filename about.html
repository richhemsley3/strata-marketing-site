Our Mission

We exist to make data management effortless — so teams can focus on building, not worrying.

Every organization deserves enterprise-grade data management. But for too long, data tools have been built for specialists — complex to deploy, difficult to maintain, and impossible to scale without a dedicated team of experts.

We're changing that. Strata gives every team the same capabilities that Fortune 500 data operations use — automated discovery, intelligent classification, real-time monitoring — wrapped in an interface anyone can operate. Great data management shouldn't be a privilege. It should be a given.

Our Values

What drives us

01

Data first, always

Data management isn't a feature we bolt on — it's the foundation everything else is built upon. We hold ourselves to the same standards we set for our customers. Every line of code, every deployment, every decision starts with one question: does this make data better governed?

02

Customer obsessed

Our customers' success is our success. We listen deeply, respond quickly, and build what matters most to the teams who rely on us every day. We don't ship features for press releases. We ship solutions for real problems.

03

Relentlessly simple

Complexity is the enemy of good data management. We obsess over making powerful capabilities feel effortless, so teams adopt better practices without friction. If it takes a manual to explain, we haven't finished designing it.

04

Transparent by default

We believe trust is earned through transparency. Our pricing, our security practices, our incident response — everything is open. Because the companies that manage data should be the most trustworthy of all.

Our Journey

From idea to industry standard

2020

The beginning

James and Amara founded Strata in San Francisco with a conviction: enterprise-grade data management shouldn't require an enterprise-sized team to operate. They wrote the first classifier in a garage office with two laptops and a whiteboard full of architecture diagrams.

2021

First enterprise customer

A Fortune 500 financial services company became our first enterprise customer, validating that Strata could handle complex, multi-cloud data environments at scale. Within 90 days, they classified 8 billion records across 14 data sources.

2023

Series A funding

Raised $28M in Series A led by Meridian Ventures to accelerate product development and go-to-market expansion. The round attracted investors who had seen firsthand the gap between legacy data tools and what modern data teams actually need.

2024

SOC 2 Type II certified

Achieved SOC 2 Type II certification, reinforcing our commitment to the highest standards of security and compliance. We don't just sell data management — we live it. Every process, every system, every vendor relationship is held to the same bar.

2026

500+ customers

Surpassed 500 customers across financial services, healthcare, technology, and government — managing over 50 billion records. What started as a conviction in a garage is now a platform trusted by some of the world's most data-conscious organizations.
